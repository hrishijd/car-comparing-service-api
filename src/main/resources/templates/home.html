Retrieve list of available car names

https://hrishis-car-rest-service.herokuapp.com/car/list

Retrieve data of available car with the help of id no.(the ID no. of name you have selected)

https://hrishis-car-rest-service.herokuapp.com/car/<no.>

Retrieve data of available car with the help of its name.

https://hrishis-car-rest-service.herokuapp.com/car/name/<name>(the name should be same from the list given)
